Document Processing Systems

Turn messy documents into clean operational records.

PDFs, spreadsheets, forms, and inbox attachments often hold the data teams need. The problem is the manual copying, checking, renaming, and retyping around them.

Crestline Ops builds systems that read the source, structure the important fields, flag uncertain values, and send clean records into the tools your team already uses.

What gets fixed

Less copying. Fewer silent errors. Faster review.

The system is designed around the actual document types you receive and the final place where the data needs to land.

Document intake

Collect PDFs, spreadsheets, scans, forms, and email attachments from inboxes, folders, or work queues.

Field extraction

Pull names, dates, prices, addresses, terms, totals, references, notes, and custom business fields into consistent columns.

Review handling

Missing values, low-confidence fields, unusual layouts, and exceptions are flagged instead of guessed silently.
