Workflow

How RankForge finds SEO-ready local businesses.

The product is designed to create fewer, higher-quality leads: businesses with SEO gaps, commercial potential, and a reachable contact path.

Search setup

An agency creates a search by entering niche, business type, city, country, and score thresholds. The request is sent into the automation workflow.

Business discovery

RankForge gathers candidates from local search sources, filters obvious junk, and keeps real business websites for review.

Website signal extraction

The workflow checks homepage and contact-path signals: title/meta quality, service pages, location pages, local SEO indicators, content depth, and website accessibility.

Contact discovery

Emails and phone numbers are extracted from homepage header/footer, contact pages, tel/mailto links, text patterns, and local listing fallback sources.

Scoring and qualification

Each candidate receives SEO need, commercial fit, contact confidence, and overall lead score. Weak records are rejected; promising records become qualified or review-needed.

Dashboard review

Users review saved lists, filter leads, inspect details, and export records for outreach. Leads without a reachable contact path are hidden from the working list.

Quality principle

Fewer, better leads beat large weak lists.

The system is intentionally designed to prioritize reachable businesses with a visible SEO reason to contact them.

Reachable

Phone or email should exist before a record becomes a working lead.

Relevant

The business should match the niche, city, and commercial intent.

Actionable

The lead detail page should explain the SEO problem and next action.

Exportable

The final list should be usable by agency operators and outreach teams.
