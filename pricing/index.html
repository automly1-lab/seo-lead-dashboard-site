FAQ

Questions agencies usually ask.

Is this a generic lead scraper?

No. RankForge is built around SEO need, commercial fit, contact readiness, and lead review workflows for SEO agencies.

Do you guarantee customers?

No. RankForge provides prioritized SEO sales opportunities, not guaranteed closed deals. Outreach, offer, and sales process still matter.

Can I export leads?

Yes. Leads can be exported for outreach review and agency workflows.

Will deep SEO audits be included?

Deep SEO audits are planned as a future on-demand credit feature. The current product focuses on lead intelligence and contact readiness.
